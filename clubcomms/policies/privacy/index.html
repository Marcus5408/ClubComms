Information We Collect

ClubComms allows users to log in using their Google credentials. When you choose to log in, the App securely collects and stores your Google log-in data, including:

Your Google log-in data is collected solely for the purpose of providing you with personalized features and enhancing your experience within the App. We do not share, sell, or transmit this information to any third parties.

It's important to note that all collected Google log-in data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not transfer or synchronize this data with any external servers or third-party platforms.

We take the security of your information seriously. The App employs industry-standard security measures to protect your Google log-in data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission or electronic storage is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Access and Control

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your Google log-in data stored within the App. You can manage these preferences directly within the App settings.

Privacy for High School Students

ClubComms is designed for use by high school students. We are committed to protecting the privacy and data of our users, including students. The App is intended for individuals of high school age and older.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 13 years old. If you are under the age of 13, please do not use this App. If you are a high school student, it is recommended that you use this App with the guidance and consent of your parents, guardians, or school administrators.

All information, including Google log-in data, is stored exclusively on your device, and the App does not transmit any data externally.

If you have any questions or concerns about how your data is handled within the App, please contact us.
